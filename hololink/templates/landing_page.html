Graph Database enlighten the path

Compared to hierarchical database, Graph database has inherent connection between data. This feature make graph database work like human brain and much suitable for storing connected thought.

Pendulum System

Which article you should read? To answer this question we build "Pendulum System" based on Hololink. When you encounter a new article we can help you analyse the article and compare the keyword with your Hololink. We will recommend you read the article which have 50% known keyword and 50% unknown keyword.

Identify the boundary of your personal knowledge system

The more connection a keyword has the bigger the influence it get. Hololink will identify the keywords connected to this major keyword but not yet discovered by you. These keywords will form your knowledge boundary

Co-work everywhere with everyone

The hardest questions need people work together to solve, we support this idea. Every Hololink project is shareable and can upload articles from different users. Team members can maintenance their knowledge base together.
